Common questions

How do I restore a purchase?

Open the app’s Settings screen and tap Restore Purchases, or reopen the paywall and tap Restore. Make sure you’re signed in with the same Apple ID you used to buy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. On your iPhone, open Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, choose the app, and tap Cancel. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription.

Where is my data stored?

Everything you create stays on your device. Our apps work offline and don’t use accounts, so your data isn’t stored on any server. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I deleted the app — can I get my data back?

Because data lives only on your device, deleting an app removes its data. We can’t recover it, so we recommend keeping iCloud device backups turned on.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in, and select the purchase.
