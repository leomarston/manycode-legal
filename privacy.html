Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 July 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications published by Emine Sarıdoğan under the Manycode name (each, an “App”). We designed our Apps to be private by default. The short version: your content stays on your device, and we do not collect it.

Information we do not collect

Our Apps do not require an account and do not ask you to sign in. We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, location, photos, or the content you create in the App (such as timers, entries, notes, reminders, or photos you add). This information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We have no servers that receive your content.

No tracking. Our Apps contain no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking. We do not build a profile of you and we do not track you across other apps or websites.

Data stored on your device

The content you create is saved locally using Apple’s on-device storage (such as SwiftData and UserDefaults). You remain in control of it. Deleting the App removes this data from your device. If the App offers an export feature, exported files are created on your device and shared only if and where you choose.

Purchases and subscriptions

If you choose to buy a subscription or one-time purchase, the transaction is handled by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment card details.

To manage entitlements (that is, to know whether your subscription is active) our Apps use RevenueCat, Inc. as a subscription-management service. When you make or restore a purchase, RevenueCat processes a randomly generated, anonymous app-user identifier together with the App Store purchase receipt and subscription status. This information is not linked to your identity and is used only to deliver the features you paid for and to restore purchases across your own devices. See RevenueCat’s privacy policy at revenuecat.com/privacy and Apple’s at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Notifications

Some Apps can send reminders. These are local notifications scheduled and delivered on your device. We do not operate push servers and do not send you messages.

Photos and camera

If an App lets you attach a photo, the image is stored locally on your device as part of your content. It is not uploaded to us or to any third party. The App requests camera or photo-library access only when you use a feature that needs it, and you can decline or revoke access in iOS Settings at any time.

Children

Our Apps are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

Because your content lives only on your device, you can view, edit, or permanently delete it at any time from within the App or by deleting the App. Since we do not hold your personal data on our side, there is nothing for us to retrieve or erase on your behalf.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our Apps evolve. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
